Two ways to sell on 1HandIndia

Retail and service businesses use separate workflows, so each seller sees the tools they need without operational clutter.

R

Best for products such as groceries, electronics, home goods, fashion, spare parts, wholesale items, and local store inventory.

  • Update fulfilment status from seller center.
  • Track payments, settlements, wallet, payouts, and statements.

Best for TV repair, phone repair, AC service, installation, maintenance, salon, consultation, cleaning, training, and similar services.

Retail seller flow

Retail sellers use the product catalogue, customer checkout, delivery status, and seller payout workflow.

Stage What seller does What platform handles Outcome
Catalogue Add product name, category, images, price, stock, tax, and variants. Validates listing quality and sends product for admin approval. Approved products appear on storefront.
Order Receives order in seller dashboard. Captures checkout details, customer payment mode, and order number. Seller starts fulfilment.
Fulfilment Accept, process, pack, dispatch, or update delivery status. Tracks timeline, delivery events, customer order status, and COD verification where applicable. Customer receives order updates.
Finance Views wallet, payout request, and statements. Calculates commission, deductions, eligible settlements, and payout status. Seller receives net payout as per payout cycle.

Service provider flow

Service providers manage bookings separately from retail orders because services need scheduling, quotes, completion proof, and dispute handling.

Stage What provider does What customer sees Outcome
Service listing Create service title, category, description, visit modes, price, inspection fee, service radius, and coverage area. Service cards with provider name, rating, price, availability, and booking action. Admin-approved service becomes public.
Booking request Receives issue details, preferred time, address, and visit mode. Clear serviceability message if location is unavailable. Provider accepts, rejects, or sends quote.
Quote and schedule Send quote, schedule visit, or mark job in progress. Can accept or reject quote before expiry. Accepted quote moves toward scheduled work.
Completion Submit completion note and proof images. Confirms completion or raises dispute. Confirmed completion unlocks settlement eligibility.
Payout Tracks service earnings and payout status. Receives service completion and payment updates. Provider payout is calculated after commission and deductions.

Service pricing modes

Choose a pricing model that matches how your work is sold.

₹

Fixed price

Use this when the service has a clear price, such as installation, cleaning package, or standard maintenance.

Q

Quote first

Use this when the final cost depends on inspection, parts, time, or issue complexity.

I

Inspection fee

Collect a visit or diagnosis fee first. If customer declines the final quote after visit, inspection fee rules apply.

Documents to keep ready

Submitting clear documents helps approval move faster.

  • Owner or authorized person ID proof.
  • PAN card or business PAN.
  • Address proof for store, office, or service base.
  • Bank proof or cancelled cheque for payouts.
  • GST certificate if registered.
  • FSSAI certificate for food sellers where applicable.
  • Business registration or authorization letter if needed.
  • Service proof, license, or certificates for regulated service categories.

Payments, commission, and payouts

1HandIndia keeps customer payment tracking and seller payout visibility clear.

P

Depending on platform settings, customers can pay using available modes such as online payment, COD, manual, bank transfer, or service pay-at-visit.

C

₹

Payouts

Eligible delivered retail orders and completed services become payable through the seller payout process.

Best practices for approval and sales

Good listings build trust and reduce support calls.

Retail sellers

  • Use clear product names and real product images.
  • Keep price, stock, tax, and variant details accurate.
  • Update order status quickly after receiving orders.
  • Pack products safely and keep customer communication professional.

Service providers

  • Write exactly what the service includes and excludes.
  • Set realistic coverage radius and visit modes.
  • Respond to booking requests and quotes quickly.
  • Submit completion notes and proof after service is done.
